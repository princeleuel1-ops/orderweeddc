# orderweeddc Product Identity

## Canonical name

The public product is `orderweeddc`, written as one lowercase word. Its
canonical domain is `orderweeddc.com`.

## Naming boundary

- Customer-facing product, navigation, metadata, evidence tools, comparison,
  documentation, and administration use `orderweeddc`.
- CANA is the internal evolution, verification, and release-safety engine.
- Internal runtime identifiers may retain `CANA` where renaming would break
  durable state, scripts, watchdog tasks, or security boundaries.
- `CANA` must not be presented as a second customer-facing product.
- Tenant brands keep their own names; the canonical tenant uses the
  `orderweeddc` wordmark.

## Wordmark

The wordmark is lowercase `orderweeddc`. The `dc` suffix may use the product
accent color, but the text, accessible name, and document metadata must remain
the complete canonical name.

## Truth boundary

Brand consistency never overrides the platform's demonstration labels,
evidence requirements, age gate, legal disclaimers, tenant isolation, or
authorization controls.
