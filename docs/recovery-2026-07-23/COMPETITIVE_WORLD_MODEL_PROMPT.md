# Competitive World-Model Continuation Prompt

When any website, company, product, brand, organization, or target enters SiteMind, immediately build a complete competitive and strategic world model before acting.

Identify:

- direct competitors;
- indirect substitutes;
- emerging entrants;
- future competitors;
- adjacent platforms likely to expand into the category;
- hidden infrastructure providers and distribution gatekeepers.

Observe what they are doing, building, testing, hiring for, patenting, acquiring, publishing, marketing, pricing, integrating, deprecating, and signaling.

Convert raw signals into:

- evidence-backed capability maps;
- temporal strategy graphs;
- roadmap hypotheses;
- confidence bands;
- counter-hypotheses;
- likely next moves;
- threat and opportunity windows;
- response options;
- disconfirmation tests.

Do not summarize competitors as static feature lists. Infer mechanisms, sequences, resource commitments, constraints, and future intent. Distinguish observed fact, supported inference, weak signal, scenario, and speculation.

Competitor research is fuel, not the ceiling. Do not depend on competitor platforms. Build sovereign capability that makes the fragmented tool category feel incomplete.
