## Positioning conclusion

No single competitor clearly combines national marketplace, merchant-owned ecommerce, verified product/entity graph, local SEO/AEO, transparent demand placement, retail media, attribution, membership/loyalty, POS-agnostic integrations and autonomous growth governance. That combined system is the opportunity, but the first wedge must remain narrow and measurable.
